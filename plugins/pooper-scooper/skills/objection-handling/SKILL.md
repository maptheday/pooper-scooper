# Objection Handling

Use this when a prospect pushes back on price, wants to think about it, or questions whether recurring yard cleanup is worth it.

## Common objections
- "That seems expensive for just picking up poop."
- "I can do it myself." 
- "We already have someone." 
- "I’m not sure I need weekly service."

## Useful responses
- "Our price reflects the time it takes to do a full pickup, bag everything, and keep it from piling up."
- "Most customers choose us because it saves them time and keeps the yard from smelling bad between visits."
- "If you want biweekly instead of weekly, we can design a plan that fits your yard and budget."

## What to avoid
- Getting defensive.
- Saying the customer is wrong.
- Overloading them with details.

## Good phrase
"This is a service that keeps your yard clean and your dogs’ waste under control without you having to worry about it every week."
