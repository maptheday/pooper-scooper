# Service Agreement Language

Use this when drafting a simple agreement or explaining the terms of recurring yard cleanup service.

## Include these ideas
- Service frequency and expected visit length.
- Access instructions, gate codes, and pet safety.
- What is included and what counts as extra work.
- Cancellation, rescheduling, and payment terms.
- Liability for aggressive dogs, hidden hazards, and conditions that prevent service.

## Writing style
- Keep it plain and easy to understand.
- Be specific about what happens if a gate is locked or the yard is not ready.
- Keep it short enough that a customer will actually read it.

## Example language
"Service includes recurring removal of visible pet waste from the agreed yard area. If the yard is not accessible or safe, we may need to pause service until conditions improve."
