# Referral Ask

Use this when you want a satisfied customer to refer their neighbors or friends who need regular yard cleanup.

## Best approach
- Ask after a good service visit.
- Keep the request simple.
- Make it easy for them to share your name or contact.

## Example
"If you know anyone nearby who needs reliable poop pickup service, I’d appreciate the referral. I’m happy to treat them to the same dependable route service you get."

## Tip
Referrals keep your route full and help you avoid discounting new customers just to win business.
