# Performance Conversation

Use this when you need to address quality, speed, or route reliability with a team member.

## Recommended approach
1. Start with the specific issue and its impact on the route.
2. Ask for their perspective.
3. Re-state the performance standard.
4. Agree on the next step.

## Examples of issues
- Leaving piles behind in a yard.
- Taking too long and falling behind the route.
- Missing locked gates or leaving notes unread.
- Not following dog handling or safety procedures.

## Tone
- Calm and firm.
- Focused on improvement, not personal blame.
- Clear about how the problem affects customers and the team.

## Example opener
"I want to talk about a few yards we missed last week and how that is affecting the route. We need to keep customers on schedule, so I want to make sure you know the standard and how to meet it."
