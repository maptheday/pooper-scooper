# Neighborhood Outreach

Use this when you want to market your pooper scooper service to nearby homes where you already work.

## Best practices
- Mention the street or neighborhood you already serve.
- Position the message as local and convenient.
- Keep the ask simple and easy to respond to.

## Suggested structure
1. Say you already do yards nearby.
2. Mention the benefit of reliable, recurring service.
3. Give them a fast way to request a quote.

## Example
"Hi neighbors — we’re already servicing homes on [street/name]. If you want a dependable poop pickup service that keeps your yard clean on the same schedule, message me and I’ll get you a quote."
