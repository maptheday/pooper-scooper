# Price Increase Notice

Use this when you need to raise rates for existing customers on a recurring yard cleanup route.

## Best practices
- Give advance notice.
- Explain the reason clearly.
- Frame it around the value of consistent, reliable service.

## Suggested wording
"Effective [date], our standard pickup rate will change from [old price] to [new price]. This helps us continue to provide dependable weekly service, keep our route staffed, and cover rising costs for labor, fuel, and supplies."

## Guardrails
- Avoid apologizing for charging a fair price.
- Keep the message short and professional.
- Offer a simple way for customers to ask questions.
